About einHaru Collective

  • einHaru Collective is a Berlin-based online boutique curating minimalist Korean and Japanese fashion for women across Europe. We source timeless pieces from independent designers in Seoul and Tokyo and ship within the EU.

  • einHaru Collective is based in Berlin, Germany. All orders are packed and shipped from Berlin via tracked services.

  • We focus on independent Korean and Japanese labels, limited runs, and long-wear pieces rather than trend-led fast fashion.

02

Products

  • We curate Korean and Japanese minimalist ready-to-wear including tops, trousers, outerwear, and everyday essentials.

  • Yes. Pieces are sourced directly from independent labels and studios in Seoul and Tokyo.

  • Some core items restock occasionally. Follow Instagram for updates.

Sizing & Fit

  • Use detailed product measurements and fit notes on each product page. If between sizes, we recommend sizing up.

  • Yes. They can run slimmer. Compare measurements directly and consult the Size Guide.

04

Shipping

  • We currently ship across the EU. For non-EU requests, contact einharu@gmail.com.

  • Shipping is free in the EU on orders over €100. Standard rates apply below that threshold.

  • All orders are dispatched from Berlin, Germany via tracked carriers.

05

Orders & Payments

  • Click Buy Now on a product page. Checkout is securely processed via Stripe.

  • Payments are handled securely through Stripe. Available card and wallet options are shown at checkout based on your location.

  • Contact us as soon as possible at einharu@gmail.com. Changes are only possible before dispatch.

06

Returns & Exchanges

  • Yes. You can request a return within 14 days of delivery for unworn items in original condition. Please contact einharu@gmail.com to start your return.

  • Email us immediately with photos and your order number so we can resolve it quickly.

  • Final sale items and damaged or worn returns are not eligible for refund.
