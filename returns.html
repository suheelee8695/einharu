At einHaru Collective, we curate each piece with care. This page outlines our return and refund policy for online clothing purchases made through einharu.com.

EU Consumer Rights

EU customers are entitled to a statutory 14-day withdrawal period for distance purchases under Directive 2011/83/EU.

01

Return Eligibility

Eligible if item is:

  • Requested within 14 days of delivery
  • Unworn and in original condition
  • Unwashed and unaltered
  • With original tags attached
  • In original packaging

Not eligible if item is:

  • Worn, washed, or altered
  • Missing original tags or packaging
  • Returned after the 14-day window
  • Marked as final sale
02

How to Start a Return

  1. 01

    Contact us

    Email einharu@gmail.com with your order number and item details within 14 days of delivery.

  2. 02

    Receive approval

    We reply with return instructions and the return address in Berlin.

  3. 03

    Ship with tracking

    Return the item in original condition and keep your tracking number until received.

  4. 04

    Refund issued

    After inspection, refunds are processed within 5 to 10 business days to your original payment method.

03

Return Shipping

Return shipping costs are the customer's responsibility for standard returns.

For items confirmed as damaged or incorrect on arrival, we review each case and provide appropriate support.

Original outbound shipping costs are non-refundable unless required by applicable law.

04

Refund Process

  • Refunds are returned to the original payment method
  • Processing time is 5 to 10 business days after inspection
  • You receive confirmation by email once processed

Bank posting timelines may vary after the refund is issued.

05

Damaged or Incorrect Items

If your order arrives damaged or incorrect, contact us as soon as possible with your order number and clear photos.

We will review and provide next steps quickly.

06

Exceptions & Special Cases

  • Final sale items are not returnable
  • Late returns cannot be accepted
  • Damaged or worn returns are not eligible for refund
07

Your EU Consumer Rights

EU customers have a statutory right of withdrawal within 14 days for distance contracts. To exercise this right, contact us clearly before the 14-day period expires.

For dispute support, you may use the EU ODR platform: ec.europa.eu/consumers/odr.
