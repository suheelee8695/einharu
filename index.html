Our name reflects our philosophy: ein (German): “one / unique” — precision, individuality, a singular vision. Haru 하루 (Korean): “day / flow of time” — renewal, freshness, the beauty of daily wear. Together, einHaru represents “one unique day” — a reminder that fashion should be both timeless and ever-renewing, grounding your everyday in quiet confidence.
