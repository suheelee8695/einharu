Our name reflects our philosophy:
ein (German): “one / unique” — precision, individuality, a singular vision.
Haru 하루 (Korean): “day / flow of time” — renewal, freshness, the beauty of daily wear.
Together, einHaru represents “one unique day” — a reminder that fashion should be both timeless and ever-renewing, grounding your everyday in quiet confidence. We are currently curating and sourcing carefully selected designer wholesale brands from Korea, building a collective that values quality, consistency, and style that lasts beyond a season.
